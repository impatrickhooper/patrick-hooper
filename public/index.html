{ ABOUT }

I love to solve problems. Preferrably using technology, but I'm not picky.

Ever since I was a little kid in the great midwestern state of Michigan, I've had an affinity for keeping things clean. But in a totally awesome way. Seriously - I used to walk around with a broom and dustpan making sure everything was up to snuff. Okay...what does this have to do with anything? Well, it's a good way to shed some light on how I approach a lot of things in my life. I like when things are neat and streamlined. I believe quality over quantity and all that jazz.


So I got myself a shiny degree from the University of Michigan in Industrial & Operations Engineering to really help me learn about efficiency, paired it with a love of web design and development, and never looked back. I think optimization + (web) technology is #sexy. Paperless, automated processes are stunning. Clean, simple designs are gorgeous. Concise, effective code is beautiful. And all of those things together? To me, that's art.


If you want to know more about where I've been and what I've done, check out my résumé and my portfolio.
