This page puts faces to names and gives more detailed information about the JMorton team. The black and white photos fit in seamlessly with the clean design and help give it a classic look and feel.

{ PROJECTS }

The project sections highlight photos and project names from the chief areas JMorton does business in. A few choice photos they felt best represented these projects were used, but not too many so the simple, unimposing feel of the site was maintained.

jmortonla.com/projects

The people at JMorton put a lot of emphasis on community. They wanted to show this and the great organizations they work with, so a news-like feed was organized to hightlight this and give more photos/information about each one.
