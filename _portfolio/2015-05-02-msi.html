{ HOME }

The home section is a hero section of sorts. It provides visitors with a full-screen slide show of images and business areas MSi lights up, from retail to hospitality to the Liberty Bell.

{% include browser.html url="msissl.com" image="portfolio_msi" extension="jpg" alt="| Home" %}

{ PRODUCT LINE }

MSi's entire product line is elegantly put on display in this section, presenting you first with a thumbnail of each item followed by more information and a larger image loaded via AJAX when an is clicked.

{% include browser.html url="msissl.com/product-line" image="portfolio_msi_product-line" extension="jpg" alt="| Product Line" %}

{ TECHNOLOGY }

This section brings a burst of color onto the page with a background composition made up of the MSi green and a few of their products. On top of this background lies a cleanly organized list of the tech MSi uses.

{% include browser.html url="msissl.com/technology" image="portfolio_msi_technology" extension="jpg" alt="| Technology" %}

{ PRESS RELEASES }

A simple, gently styled list of news and press releases is displayed in this section. Nothing too over-the-top, just enough to be appealing and allow you to focus on what's important here: the content.

{% include browser.html url="msissl.com/press-releases" image="portfolio_msi_press-releases" extension="jpg" alt="| Press Releases" %}

{ SALES KIT }

MSi also has a wealth of additional information about each product and many other resources like presentations and rebate information that clients find useful. The trick with all this information on a website was organizing it in a way that is intutive and simple. The answer? Google Images-style drawers and accordions inside of those to hide and reveal information as necessary!
